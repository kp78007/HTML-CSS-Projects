Our mission is to familarize the world with the vast riches of modern South Asia.

From the mountains of Pakistan, to the deserts of India, to the rivers of Bangladesh, lie

numerous small villages with talented innovators who are reshaping the economic structure of

the region. We want to show the world the value these village dwellers hold in the making of

contemporary South Asia and shed a light on their beautiful creations.
